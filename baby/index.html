Logan and Lisa are having a baby!


Baby Pebblemore will be arriving sometime around July 14, 2021 and we’re so incredibly excited

The story!

We both spent November 3rd, 2020 volunteering for seventeen hour shifts as poll workers during the General Election. Since we were stationed at two separate polling sites, we didn’t see each other all day (for probably the first time in about three years, thanks to vanlife and the pandemic!). The next afternoon, after an extremely long night of rest, we were about to head out for an outdoor lunch. As we were getting ready to leave, Lisa noticed that she was feeling a little dizzy and had realized that looking back she’d been feeling that way for a week or so. On a hunch, she took a pregnancy test and we were both so thrilled when we saw a very definitive little plus sign confirm that she was indeed pregnant! Ever since, we’ve been excitedly preparing to meet the newest member of our family and we can’t wait to introduce you, too!

We have very specifically decided to not find out the sex of the baby (including shielding our eyes during all ultrasounds when the helpful nurse tells us to!). We’re doing our best to keep it a complete secret until the birth!

Oh, and what’s the story behind the nickname Baby Pebblemore? A good friend of ours was extremely elated when he found out that we are expecting and sleepily muttered to his partner right before bed “Logan and Lisa are having a baby! It’s a Pebblemore!” It was delightful, we absolutely love it and it’s become Baby Rockmore’s nickname ever since.

Photos and videos!

Rather than oversharing on social media, we are going to be using a website called Tiny Beans. Using it, we can privately share photos and videos of our little one to all of our family and friends. They have an app that you can use, but it’s not necessary and you can easily receive updates via email if you’d prefer. We’ve already started adding some ultrasound and baby bump photos there and we’re super excited to share all of our newborn photos once we have them!

If you’re interested in joining Tiny Beans, please provide us with your information and we will send you an invite.

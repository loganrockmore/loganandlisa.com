Hi everyone!

First of all, we're so excited to celebrate with everybody and we really appreciate that you want to get us a gift to commemorate the occasion. The time, effort, and expense of traveling to be with us is something we deeply appreciate, and we are so thrilled that we will be able to celebrate this happy occasion with you!

So instead of physical gifts, we've decided that we would like to raise money for a few charities that are doing work that we believe in. These organizations can really use our support and we're exciting about taking this opportunity to help them out!

For those that are instead looking to buy something specifically for us, we have listed a few of the things that we know that we'll need for our van trip. Purchasing those from our registry website will provide us with the cash equivalent of your gift and we'll be able to purchase the item ourselves as we're on the road. We also have some more traditional wedding gifts listed, which we won't be purchasing now but will be getting once we move back into a home after this adventure of ours. We kindly request that you use the registry website instead of buying physical gifts.

You'll notice that all of the gifts here are broken down into smaller price increments. We did that to make it easier for you to contribute at whatever amount you'd like, so please feel free to buy a part of any gift or the entire thing. Just make sure to select the right number of portions when choosing any item.

Thank you again for supporting us and our love, and we're so excited to see you at the wedding celebration!

— Lisa and Logan
